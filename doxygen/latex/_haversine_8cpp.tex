Calculate the great-\/circle distance between two points on the Earth specified by their latitude and longitude using the Haversine formula. 

This function computes the shortest distance over the Earth\textquotesingle{}s surface, giving an "{}as-\/the-\/crow-\/flies"{} distance between the points (ignoring any hills, valleys, or other obstacles).
